About

The Name's Niall Gerety, my drifting career started back in 2016 when I purchased my first E36. This car was a 2.0 which was quickly swapped out for a 2.5. After out driving this car it was time to change up to the E46 M3 3.2 s54. This car is my current competition car. Competing in drift championship in both Ireland and England. I currently have a YouTube channel niallgoesdrifting where we build drift cars and follow the car scene in Ireland.
